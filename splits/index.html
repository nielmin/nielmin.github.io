⌨️-⌨️: My Split Keyboards

Current Split

I built a Ferris Sweep Bling MX in June 2026. This is currently my endgame (if one even exists).

I realized that Choc v1 switches cannot compare to MX switches in terms of typing experience and cost.

Additionally, I do not benefit from the compactness and portability of my small keyboards.

So moving forward, I will only be building MX keyboards.

Previous Splits
